## üîç HOW AI SEARCH ENGINES WORK

### **ChatGPT Search:**
- **Prioritizes**: Comprehensive answers, authoritative sources
- **Loves**: FAQ schema, detailed explanations, citations
- **Avoids**: Thin content, keyword stuffing, poor formatting

### **Perplexity AI:**
- **Prioritizes**: Current information, cited sources
- **Loves**: Timely content, data-backed analysis, references
- **Avoids**: Outdated information, unsourced claims

### **Claude Search:**
- **Prioritizes**: Ethical content, balanced perspectives
- **Loves**: Well-structured arguments, multiple viewpoints
- **Avoids**: Biased content, sensationalism

### **Google SGE:**
- **Prioritizes**: E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- **Loves**: Comprehensive coverage, user intent matching
- **Avoids**: Low-quality content, manipulative tactics
